Who we are

Home for Our Troops is a private non-profit organization that caters specifically to injured post 9/11 veterans to help rebuild their lives. Most of these courageous individuals have sustained injuries such as amputated injuries, Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and brain trauma. By contributing to this organization you will be helping veterans like these to receive some of the freedom and independence they lost after sacrificing them defending our country. Any donations received will automatically help the building of homes, and rehabilitation of veterans into highly productive members of society once more. What we're hosting

The event hosted by and for Home for Our Troops will be the building of homes in the Greenville/Simpsonville area. Volunteers may sign up and help build homes for veterans alongside our amazing staff. Tasks may include gathering materials, nail boards together, paint houses as well as beautify the surroundings once the houses are finished. As a thanks for your collaboration we will allow each volunteer to imprint their hands in the front of the house to commemorate their hard work as well as a token of gratitude towards the veteran.

To be able to participate you must sign up (and you can do so here). A t-shirt will be mailed to you and must be worn in order to participate. The cost per t-shirt is 5 dollars. We will send you extra information through e-mail so please make sure to provide one as you sign up.
